About evilpresentation

Why bother spending ages choosing and formatting slides for that big presentation when the power of the internet (mostly monkeys on speed) can do it for you? evilpresentation pulls photos from Flickr tagged appropriately, and builds a presentation out of them.

Or, why not substitute a rival's presentation for an evil one at the last minute? Just another weapon in your sneaky arsenal against those hard-working fast-climbers.

The gritty details

If you want it more customised, you can add in additional information such as your name and affiliation, and the title of your presentation. This goes into the URI itself; the following are accepted.

  • http://evilpresentation.com/presentation/[author]
  • http://evilpresentation.com/presentation/[author];[affiliation]
  • http://evilpresentation.com/presentation/[author];[affiliation]/[title]

We use Flickr "machine tags" to identify the images available. You should tag possible opening images with evil:purpose=start, general-purpose images with evil:purpose=middle, and ending photos with evil:purpose=end. All photos should have a Creative Commons license that allows commercial use. You will be attributed in a presentation for any images used.

Status

This is very much pre-release, so please do not spread around yet. In particular, there's an approach I want to take which will reduce the load on Flickr and also make the final presentations bookmarkable.
